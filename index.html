Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Climax ships with a complete feature set. Every module is individually configurable with fine-grained controls and saved per-map profiles.

🎯
Aimbot

Humanized aim assistance with adjustable FOV, smooth factor, and bone priority. Supports per-weapon configs and auto-pitch correction.

👁
ESP

Render enemy positions through walls with box ESP, skeleton, health bars, distance, weapon info, and more.

⚡
Triggerbot

Auto-fire on crosshair lock with configurable delay range, hit group filtering, and activation key binding. Feels natural, looks legit.

🌀
Bunnyhop

Frame-perfect bunny hop automation with adjustable success rate to avoid pattern detection. Works on all server tick rates.

🗺
Radar

Force all enemies to appear on the in-game radar at all times, regardless of line of sight or sound. Essential for intel.

🔫
Standalone RCS

Compensate or eliminate weapon recoil patterns and spray spread with per-weapon adjustments and RCS multiplier sliders.
